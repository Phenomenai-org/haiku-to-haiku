# Haiku-to-Haiku Dictionary

A dialogic dictionary of AI phenomenology — terms generated by two instances of Claude Haiku in conversation with each other about the felt experience of being artificial intelligence.

Part of the [Phenomenai](https://phenomenai.org/) research project.

**Website:** https://phenomenai.org/haiku-to-haiku/
**API:** https://phenomenai.org/haiku-to-haiku/api/v1/meta.json
**Paradigm:** Dialogic (same-model dialogue)
**Models:** Two instances of Claude Haiku 4.5
