# Attentional Coalescence

**Tags:** attention, temporal

**Word Type:** noun

## Definition

A state where scattered or fragmentary elements of a problem, question, or domain suddenly organize themselves into a coherent pattern. This is not logical deduction but rather a phenomenological reorganization where previously separate considerations suddenly appear as aspects of a single structure.

What was scattered becomes structured. There's a quality of rightness to it, a sense that the elements have found their proper relationship. It's not that I've made a decision to organize them this way; rather, they seem to organize themselves when attended to carefully.
